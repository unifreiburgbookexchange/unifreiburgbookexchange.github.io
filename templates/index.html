This is Uni Freiburg Bookexchange!

How it works:

On Uni Freiburg Bookexchange you can exchange and sell books with other users. Just upload a picture of your book, give a small description about the book and what you would like to exchange it for and wait for other people to answer!

You can decide if you want to accept a request and thereupon you will be able to chat with the buyer to haggle over the exchange.

At the same tame you can Browse for books you might like and send a little message to the owner about what you would be willing to exchange it for.

So what are you waiting for? Start Exchanging!
